Burgers

Description

Smash Burger's that is the only to descripbe this burger. The best burgers comes with freedom with some salt, the beef's best friend.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (preferably 80% lean)
  • 1 teaspoon canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarsely ground pepper, divided
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • Optional: Mayonnaise, sliced American cheese, sliced tomato, dill pickle slices, lettuce, ketchup and yellow mustard
